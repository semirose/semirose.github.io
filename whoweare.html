    The Team

    We have ten people on our team with us. The people on our team are great, they are supportive and fun. We all work together, and we don't get left out

    Semira

    Hello, I am 11 years old and when I met Fiona at a chess club, I knew we would be friends for life. When I swiched schools I was sad about leaving her, but, lo and behold, she join Seabury School the next year, and then we moved to the middle school campus together after I skipped 4th grade so we were in the same spot. When I invited Fiona to Startup Weekend, she accepted with out delay, but she had a lacrosse game on Saturday, but we weren't going to pitch, so it was OK. Then somebody gave as a pitch about pitching, and we pitched an idea about a video game that taught kids how to code. We formed a team, which was more than we expected, and then, among 9 other groups of adults, our team won Startup Weekend Seattle on the weekend of the 8-11th!
