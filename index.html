    The Idea

    Hello, my name is Semira and I'm 11 years old. I'm Fiona and I'm 12 years old. We realized there are 1 million coding or developer jobs that are unfilled due to lack of coders We need to start teaching kids how to code at younger ages, but when you look, as a middle-schooler, for resouces to do that, you find an overwheming lack of middle-school geared options. They have lots of games that teach drag-and-drop which is fun and can be great for exposing kids younger, but overall has very few real life implications. You also find coding that is type-based, but at the same time, it can get tedious. What Generation CODE's solution involes is a game that teaches code. What is this wonderful solution? CodeMonsters! It's a fun, interesting game that keeps kids attention so they can learn the code.
